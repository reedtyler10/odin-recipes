Spicy Dry Rub Wings

A flavorful Garlic Butter Shrimp and Rice recipe with a small kick of spice. Perfect for meal prep for your weekly lunch!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet, season with paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook until the shrimp turn pink, usually 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the cooked shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the remaining butter and cooked rice. Stir well to combine and heat through.
  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges if desired.
Enjoy your delicious Gerlic Butter Shrimp and Rice!
