BBQ Ribs

Here's a delicious BBQ ribs recipe for you!

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ribs:
    • Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs.
    • In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt to create the dry rub.
  2. Apply the Dry Rub:
    • Rub the dry rub mixture evenly over both sides of the ribs, pressing it into the meat.
    • Wrap the ribs in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight to let the flavors meld.
  3. Preheat the Grill or Smoker:
    • Preheat your grill or smoker to a temperature of about 225-250°F (107-121°C) using indirect heat.
  4. Cook the Ribs:
    • Place the ribs on the grill or smoker, bone side down.
    • Maintain a consistent temperature and smoke the ribs for 3-4 hours or until the meat easily pulls away from the bones.
  5. Prepare the BBQ Sauce Glaze:
    • In a small saucepan, mix together BBQ sauce, apple cider vinegar, and Dijon mustard.
    • Simmer over low heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Glaze the Ribs:
    • During the last 30 minutes of cooking, baste the ribs with the BBQ sauce glaze every 10 minutes, allowing it to caramelize on the surface.
  7. Rest and Server:
    • Once the ribs are done, remove them from the grill, and let them rest for a few minutes.
    • Slice between the bones and serve with extra BBQ sauce on the side.
Enjoy your delicious BBQ ribs!
